Pizza Dough

Spatula's recipe. Image from https://sugarspunrun.com/the-best-pizza-dough-recipe/

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 1 packet yeast (~2/3 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1.5 cups (320g) warm water
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Add yeast, a small amount of water, and sugar to a bowl.
  2. Let mixture sit until signs of carbonation appear.
  3. Add remaining ingredients in a large mixer bowl.
  4. Knead with mixer (using dough hook) on medium speed for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Let rise until doubled in size.
  6. Split dough into two equal parts and flatten each into the desired shape. Makes two 12-14" pizza crusts.
